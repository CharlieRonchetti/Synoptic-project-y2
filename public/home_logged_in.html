Our System

As a team of five we have come together to create a prototype system for the people of the Cape York peninsula of Australia to help with the transport issues they face over a year and especially through the monsoon season around the area. Our system is a route planner which allows the users to input a starting location and an end location with a route being displayed on a map, allowing the user to have reliable and safe directions to their destination. The system intends to include weather statistics, which can be viewed specifically for their route, allowing them to make judgements on their willingness to travel if the conditions are not satisfactory. Along with these features, our system intends to include an emergency SOS button built into it allowing the users to send their current location to emergency services should they come into trouble when travelling around Cape York, as well as a discussion board where all the users of the system can communicate among each other on the system to post updates on the accessibility of different routes as well as request lifts to and from different destinations.
